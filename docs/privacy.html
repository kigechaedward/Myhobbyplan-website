Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

Welcome to MyHobbyPlan

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MyHobbyPlan collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MyHobbyPlan is designed to respect your privacy by minimizing data collection.

  • No Account Required: We do not require users to create an account to use the application.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details.
  • Local Storage: All hobbies, schedules, reminders, and settings you create are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

2. Location Information

  • MyHobbyPlan does not track your precise GPS location.
  • To provide weather forecasts, you manually select or enter your preferred city. This information is used only to retrieve weather data and is stored locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to enhance application functionality:

  • Weather Data: We use providers (such as OpenWeather) to retrieve forecasts. Only the city name you select is sent to these providers.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. These providers may collect limited, non-personal device identifiers to deliver relevant ads.
  • Analytics: We may use anonymous analytics to track app performance and crashes to improve the user experience.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored locally by the application. However, please be aware that no software system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

MyHobbyPlan is a family-friendly application suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be published within the application and on this website.
