Privacy Policy

MyHobbyPlan

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

Welcome to MyHobbyPlan

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MyHobbyPlan collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MyHobbyPlan is designed to respect your privacy.

  • We do not require users to create an account.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information such as:
    • Name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Home address
    • Payment information
  • The hobbies, schedules, reminders, and settings you create are stored locally on your device.

2. Location Information

  • MyHobbyPlan does not collect or track your precise location.
  • To provide weather forecasts, you manually select or enter your preferred city. This city is used only to retrieve weather information and is stored locally on your device.

3. Weather Services

  • The app retrieves weather information from a trusted third-party weather provider (such as OpenWeather).
  • Only the selected city is sent to the weather service to retrieve forecasts.
  • No personal information is shared with the weather provider.

4. Advertising

  • MyHobbyPlan displays advertisements to support free access to the application.
  • Third-party advertising providers (such as Google AdMob) may collect limited, non-personal information in accordance with their own privacy policies to deliver and measure advertisements.
  • We encourage users to review Google's Privacy Policy for more information regarding advertising services.

5. Analytics

  • MyHobbyPlan may use anonymous analytics to understand application performance, identify crashes, and improve user experience.
  • These analytics do not identify individual users.

6. Data Storage

  • Your hobbies and app preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload your hobby information to our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

  • MyHobbyPlan is a family-friendly application suitable for users of all ages.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored by the application. However, no software system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
  • Any changes will be published within the application and on our website.
