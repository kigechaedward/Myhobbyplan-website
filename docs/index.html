About MyHobbyPlan

Life is better when you make time for the things you love. MyHobbyPlan was created to help people spend more time enjoying their hobbies instead of worrying about planning them.

The app combines intelligent scheduling with weather-aware planning, making it easier to choose the right time for outdoor activities while keeping all your hobbies organized in one place.

Key Features

Hobby Management

Create, edit, organize, and manage all your hobbies in one intuitive application.

Smart Scheduling

Plan activities by date and time with an easy-to-use planner that keeps you on track.

Weather-Aware Planning

View current weather and forecasts for your selected city to determine the best time for outdoor hobbies.

Personalized Preferences

Set ideal conditions for each hobby—temperature, rain, and wind—to receive custom recommendations.

Smart Notifications

Receive reminders for scheduled hobbies and alerts when weather conditions are ideal.

Family-Friendly

A clean, simple, and welcoming interface designed for users of all ages.
