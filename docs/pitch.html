The Problem

Hobbies Shouldn't Be This Hard to Maintain

Millions of people struggle to maintain hobbies and outdoor activities due to poor planning, busy schedules, and unpredictable weather. Existing tools don't solve the whole problem.

😰

Busy Schedules

People want to enjoy hobbies but struggle to find time. Without structured planning, hobbies are quickly abandoned as life gets busier.

🌧️

Unpredictable Weather

Outdoor hobbies depend on weather conditions, but most people check forecasts on separate apps with no connection to their hobby plans.

📱

Fragmented Tools

Calendar apps don't understand hobbies. Weather apps don't know your preferences. Habit trackers ignore weather entirely. No single app combines them.

😔

Missed Opportunities

Perfect weather windows for outdoor activities pass by unnoticed. Users miss ideal conditions because they're not actively monitoring forecasts.

👥

Family Coordination

Families struggle to plan shared hobby time. There's no simple tool that helps coordinate activities across different schedules and preferences.

🔄

No Personalization

Existing apps offer generic experiences. They don't adapt to individual weather preferences, hobby priorities, or personal scheduling patterns.

Our Solution

The All-in-One Hobby Planning Platform

MyHobbyPlan combines scheduling, weather forecasting, reminders, and intelligent planning into one beautiful app — giving users the power to enjoy their hobbies more.

Plan Smarter. Enjoy More.

MyHobbyPlan is designed around a simple insight: hobbies thrive when they're planned with context. By combining your hobby schedule with real-time weather data, we help you make better decisions about when and how to enjoy your activities.

  • ✓ Organize all hobbies in one intuitive dashboard
  • ✓ Get real-time weather forecasts for your activity locations
  • ✓ Set personalized weather preferences for each hobby
  • ✓ Receive smart notifications when conditions are perfect
  • ✓ Plan weekly and monthly activities with a visual planner
  • ✓ Share and coordinate hobby plans with family members
🎯

Context-Aware Planning

Your hobbies, your weather, your schedule — all in sync.

Product Features

Built for Hobbyists Who Love the Outdoors

A carefully crafted feature set that addresses every aspect of hobby planning and weather-aware scheduling.

Hobby Management

Create, edit, and organize all your hobbies in one intuitive dashboard. Categorize by type, set priorities, and track your engagement over time.

Weather-Based Planning

View current weather and forecasts for your selected city. Get real-time data on temperature, rain, wind, and humidity to plan outdoor activities.

Weekly & Monthly Planner

Plan your hobby activities week by week or month by month with a visual calendar. Drag, drop, and reschedule with ease.

Weather Preferences

Set ideal conditions for each hobby — temperature range, rain probability, wind speed — for customized activity recommendations.

Smart Notifications

Receive timely reminders for scheduled hobbies and intelligent alerts when weather conditions match your ideal hobby preferences.

Family-Friendly Experience

Designed for users of all ages with a clean, simple, and welcoming interface. Coordinate hobby plans with family members easily.
